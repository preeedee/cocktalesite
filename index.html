Starter

Cocktail Starters

The starter is the principal ingredient of the cocktail. It is typically a single spirit such as rum, gin, or whiskey, and typically makes up to 75 percent or more of the total volume of the cocktail before icing.

Starter Choices

Choose your starter wisely! Whether you love a cocktail is primarily decided by two factors: the starter and the flavors so it's good to play safe with one and experiment with the other. Different starters are enhanced by different flavors, so this acts as a good first step to narrowing down the flavor choices that you can experiment with.
